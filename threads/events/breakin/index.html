Break In

The phrase Break-In has sinister connotations.
It immediately conjures images of thieves sneaking in under the cover of darkness, people picking locks, scouting locations and at times all out gun-point aided occupation of your local sanctuary.
How much fun would it be if you could do all that thrill-seeking stuff without ever having to worry about being caught? Well now you can! Digitally!
Break-In is IIIT-Hyderabad's premier security competition, you will compete with hackers and security enthusiasts from around the world to secure the top spot in this Jeopardy style Capture the Flag event. And believe us when we say capture, for it will not be handed to you on a silver platter. Participants will have to crawl the depths of the web, pry open encrypted exchanges and reverse engineer binaries to finally emerge victorious.
Only the most skilled shall win!

The event has ended.

Rules

  1. The contest will be a Jeoperdy style CTF.
  2. There will be 5 categories of problems, with each problem having different scores based on difficulty.
  3. Each problem will involve a challenge that leads to a key, which leads to succesful completion of problem.
  4. Cases of malpractices and plagiarism will be dealt seriously. The final decision shall be at organizer's discretion.
