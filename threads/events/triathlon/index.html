Triathlon

Threads 2014 invites all to the Triwizard Tournament of Systems Programming.
The Threads Triathlon is an onsite contest that puts 3 challenges to the participants.
The challenges are open-ended tasks, that involve deep knowledge of the principles and design of the Holy Trinity of Systems Software: Operating Systems, Database Systems, and Compilers.
We cordially invite you to be a part of this celebration of programming, and make it better than before.

Rules

  1. This is an individual onsite contest.
  2. Task 1: Demoscening. What is Demoscene?
  3. Further tasks will be revealed as the event approaches.
