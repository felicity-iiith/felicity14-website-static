Kode Da Circuit

“Yesterday’s science is today’s engineering, and today’s electronics is tomorrow’s computer science.”
Those who program by bits and bytes often overlook the power of the invisible wires that bind their ideas. The burden of designing the hardware to realise most elegant ideas of Computer Science, rests on the shoulders of those blessed with the art of conceiving engines that compute.Thus, we invite the spiritual successors of Charles Babbage to take on the challenges that plague the world of electronics today.
Kode Da Circuit is an unique online VHDL programming competition wherein participants are expected to develop codes to simulate hardware. Questions may vary from designing simple combinational circuits to debugging a given VHDL code to designing a system for hardware acceleration. A week long practice session will be conducted before the competition so as to acquaint the participants with the online judge, submission format and scoring system.
