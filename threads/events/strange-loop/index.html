Strange Loop

Can you rescue yourself from the Combinatorial explosion?
Can you shield yourself from the Curse of dimensionality?
Or are you destined to be haunted by the demons of intractability?
A Knight is one who faces all challenges, even those that the common might deem impossible. So are you one who is afraid of complex puzzles, gigantic algebras, or intricate games, and the one who can ward off the ghosts of intractability?
Strange Loop belongs to a new genre of AI and combinatorial search contests, and will contain challenges from puzzles like Sudoku, Kakuro, Rubik's Cube, and games like Reversi, for you to play with and devise effective search strategies and heuristics for constraint satisfaction and game playing.
Strange Loop will consist of three challenges, each with different types of complexities to tackle, and invites everyone with interest in game AI, search optimization, or any field of computer science that deals with study of puzzles and games to come up with problem solving strategies.
