Tale Spin

“Que Sera, Sera”

He was the Captain of Casablanca
Nomad aboard the sea of doom.
They say it was her sighs of wait,
That spun the words, and brought them together.

A simple string of words spun together can create something beautiful. So, take some time to spin your own tale, and express your feelings and desires. Because we do know, blessed are those who can express within the finite. So let the O.Henry and the Sidney Porter in you flow free in this short story competition!

You may use either one of the following two options to base your story on:
  1. At the stroke of the midnight hour, as they slept, she awoke. She walked towards the light outside the window ....
    OR




  • Each participant may submit a maximum of two stories.
  • Your story should be between 500-2000 words. Each word outside the limit will be accordingly penalized.
  • Most importantly, kindly submit only real, original work. Plagiarized entries would automatically be disqualified.

    Prizes:
    1st Position : Rs 1500
    2nd Position: Rs 1000

    • Rules

    1. If you choose to work on Part A, kindly keep in mind the following guidelines.
      You are allowed to make the following changes to the above lines so as to account for your style of writing:-
      • POV (first person, third person, second person!)
      • Tense used (past simple, present etc)
      • Change the style or structure of the above sentences such that it aligns with the rest of your story.
      • Add descriptive comments (adjectives, metaphors etc) to add a flavour to it.
      • The most important thing is that your modified lines should stay true to the essence of the given ones.
    2. If you choose to work on part B, there is really just one basic rule. Your story should derive its inspiration from the image provided. Let your imagination do the rest of the work.
