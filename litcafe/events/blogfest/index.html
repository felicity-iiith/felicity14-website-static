AOL CoolAge BlogFest

 CoolAge will host AOL CoolAge Blogfest, a live blogging event that will stretch over 2 days of Felicity. Students can blog on any one of the three topics that will be given on the spot.

There will be 3 winners for each day, the prizes are:
1st place: iPod+ AOL CoolAge goodie bag + induction into the AOL CoolAge blogger program
2nd place: Philips Headphones + AOL CoolAge goodie bag
3rd place: 16 GB Pen drive + AOL CoolAge goodie bag
The entries will be judged by the AOL CoolAge editorial team, and the winners will be notified and prizes given out at the end of the day

Rules

  • Clarity of thought
  • Word count – Minimum: 300, Maximum: 400
  • Articulate, right use of grammar & vocabulary
  • Obscenity, profanity, derogatory, defamatory, blasphemous, racial or such other negative blogs will not qualify
