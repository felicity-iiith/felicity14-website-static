KryptoKnight
Do you know the stuff that superhumans are made of? Are the superhumans you've grown up with a little too obsolete for the world today? Here's your chance, dear human, to create an extraordinary being. Create and we shall unleash your superhumans upon each other in a fair battleground. And the victorious shall stand rewarded!
Be warned though, that "with great power, comes great responsibility!"

Prizes!
1st place - Rs. 1500/-
2nd place - Rs. 1000/-

Here's one from our side:
Captain Chemical Nemesis:

Superpowers:
1. Can fly.
2. Can control bacteria.
3. None.

Weaknesses:
1. Ayurvedic herbs
2. Chocolates
3. None.

Rules

  • You're allowed to make, design, think, come up with a maximum of two different characters - one super villain of your choice and one super hero.
  • You need to post a detailed description of your character with or without a sketch - at the maximum 15 powers, and weaknesses combined.
  • You're allowed to EITHER only sketch your character, appropriately defining the powers, weaknesses of your character in the sketch itself, OR provide a description. Make your character as wacky as you can.
    Be it The Examiner, The Invigilator, Fancy Girl, or anything. Get crazy.
    Your character will also be judged on how complete it is and the extent to which the superpowers and weaknesses complement each other.The maddest, wackiest and most well-rounded character wins.
