Free Verse
"  Twelve people waiting in suspension
With bent knees,
And clasped hands;
An eerie silence fills the court
As the server makes her move
And with a
Bump,
Set,
Spike,
The ball makes its target,
And a point is scored.  "

Who said a poem needs to rhyme?

Anybody can be a free verse poet. Free verse poetry is liberating, from all the set orthodox rules of poetry. All you need, my friend, is a story moulded into a few beautifully succinct lines.

Prizes!

2nd place - Rs. 1000/-

Rules

  • The judge's decision is final and no correspondence will be entered into.
