Ultimate Pulse

Enter the City of Electronics in the World of Knowledge.
Put up a show of your intelligence and pick up knowledge from all around you by catching signals from every possible system. Expand your insight by wandering the streets of transistors, diodes, robots, antennas and filters. All this through a series of challenges that will never cease to intrigue you!
Answer a test for starters. Next, design a circuit that is asked for, and then innovate a perfectly working model.

Rules

  • The contest will be take place in two stages:-
    1. Quiz Pulse
    2. Design Pulse
  • Quiz Pulse Rules:-
  1. The contest will be an on site written test.
  2. The contest will be 2 hours long.
  3. The quiz will contain questions testing general knowledge in digital and analog electronics, as well as computer architecture.
  4. The answers will be awarded marks as per the difficulty of the questions.
  5. Top Students will enter into 2nd Round of Ultimate Pulse.
  • Design Pulse Rules
  1. The contest will be an onsite circuit making competition.
  2. The contest will be 3 hours long.
  3. A Problem Statement checking your Combinational and Sequential logic will be given. You must design and make a working circuit on breadboard.
  4. Participants will be graded for their design as well as their implementation.
  5. Top participants will enter into the 3rd round of Ultimate Pulse.
