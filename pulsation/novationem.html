Novationem

Are you passionate about a cause and have a hardware solution in mind? A solution that could change the world? The one idea that could solve a problem for everyone? Well, here's your chance to present your idea, and win exciting prizes!
All you need to do is find a way to implement your idea as an electronic circuit, with a proper design and flawless functioning.
The ideas will be forwarded to interested people and companies and if you're lucky, you'll get a shot at revolutionizing the world with your idea.

Rules

  1. Be original and present a solution to any real life problem which you think is a pressing issue. Pitch your problem statement and present a solution to it. We'll see you there!
  2. Send your entries to pulsation@felicity.iiit.ac.in latest by 11:59pm on 22nd Feb '14, and we'll reply ASAP, and call you to present your solution on 23rd Feb to everyone!
  3. The entry must be in the form of a Powerpoint Presentation and a pdf report.
  4. The entry must include the names of your team members, main problem statement, your solution to it, a block level design and a circuit design you have implemented/plan to implement.
  5. The more detailed the hardware implementation, the more points will be given.
