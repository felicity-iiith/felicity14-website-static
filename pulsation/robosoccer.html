Robo Soccer

Get on the field, unleash yourself and sport. All this without getting your hands dirty. A challenge for your mind rather than body. You get to tackle a player instead of actually running around. Make a goal without having to kick the ball yourself.
In this game of soccer, Make a bot that plays soccer the best or we have Robots that do it for you. With the controller in your hand, you can command them to do all your dirty work.

Rules

  1. Play in teams of 2 against other teams.
  2. Compete against your friends to score the most goals for your team with your robot.
