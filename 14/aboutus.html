where insta meets gram

Felicity, a word synonymous with bliss, euphoria and happiness means so much more to us, IIITians. The three days of the festival bring with themselves, a wave of vitalizing energy, enthusiasm and celebration, the reverberations of which are felt throughout the campus.

We at IIIT, believe in celebrating freedom, revelling in the joys of youth and showcasing our gratitude towards independence of mind and soul. Felicity, as it stands today is a conglomeration of all these cherished beliefs and ideas.

Felicity 2014

The villages of India have always been the keepers of its cultural vitality. They safeguard the rich treasure of Indian heritage. From the daintiness of multi-hued bangles to the robustness of the Indian turban, from the silkiness of the tassels to the sharpness of the spices and from the orange of the saffron to the crimson and gold of the veils: all come together and to life in the Vibrant Villages of India.

With the soaring grandeur of a palace, India has many such vibrant corridors and passageways which crave their countless stories be told and retold. This year let us embark on a journey through these forgotten passageways to discover the tales of our identities and origins.
